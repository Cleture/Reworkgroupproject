Modern Technology

The 2024 Grand Highlander will come with an array of advanced safety features,
including lane-keeping assist, adaptive cruise control,
and automatic emergency braking. It will also have a rearview
camera and blind spot monitoring system to help you stay safe on the road.
Additionally, the SUV will come with Toyota
Safety Sense 2.0, which is an upgraded version of Toyota’s
suite of driver-assist technologies that includes
pre-collision warning and pedestrian detection.

The 2024 Grand Highlander will be powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that
produces 295 horsepower and 263 lb-ft of torque.
This engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that helps
provide smooth shifting and improved fuel economy.
The SUV also comes with an available all-wheel drive system for enhanced
traction in inclement weather condition

The Grand Highlander comes with a host of modern technology features
that make it easier to stay connected while on the go. It has an 8-inch touchscreen display with
Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration for easy access to your favorited apps and music streaming services.
The SUV also comes with Amazon Alexa compatibility so you can control your vehicle’s
climate settings or navigation system using voice commands. The Toyota Grand Highlander 2024 is sure to be a hit with families looking for a reliable and feature-packed SUV.
With its advanced safety features, improved performance, and modern technology,
it’s sure to make life on the road easier and more enjoyable for everyone involved.
