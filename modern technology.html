Modern Technology

The Grand Highlander comes with a host of modern technology features
that make it easier to stay connected while on the go. It has an 8-inch touchscreen display with
Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration for easy access to your favorited apps and music streaming services.
The SUV also comes with Amazon Alexa compatibility so you can control your vehicle’s
climate settings or navigation system using voice commands. The Toyota Grand Highlander 2024 is sure to be a hit with families looking for a reliable and feature-packed SUV.
With its advanced safety features, improved performance, and modern technology,
it’s sure to make life on the road easier and more enjoyable for everyone involved.
